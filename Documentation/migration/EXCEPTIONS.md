# Migration exceptions

Four links on the public Digital Filtering overview point to PmWiki pages that
do not exist in the downloaded source:

- FFT
- FIR vs IIR
- Second Order Sections
- Practical Considerations

Their visible labels and descriptions are retained as plain text. No replacement
content was invented. The route manifest records each item under
`exceptions.unpublished_link_targets`.

The two copied but currently unreferenced images are retained because they are
public GNC assets and appear to be alternate illustrations for the published PID
documentation:

- `pid_fopdt.png`
- `pid_pidsetrampretune.png`
